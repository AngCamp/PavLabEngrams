Attmepts at making a classifier for engram cells, previously activated cells.  Using data from studies
lsited in Fuentes-Ramos, M., Alaiz-Noya, M., & Barco, A. (2021). Transcriptome and epigenome analysis
 of engram cells: Next-generation sequencing technologies in memory research. Neuroscience & Biobehavioral Reviews.


EngrmaGEOs.txt contains the geo accession numbers of the studies with data posted to geo as well
as URL's to other metadata.

Classsifier.r is script for first attempt at a random forest classifier using Jeager and Lacar data.

FetchingData.R is just a script contiaining the locations of the data and the lines to call them.
